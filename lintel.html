LINTELS

Understanding Lintels in Construction

Lintels are horizontal structural elements placed over openings such as doors and windows to support the load from the structure above. They are essential components in both residential and commercial buildings, ensuring that loads are transferred safely to the surrounding walls or columns.

Lintels can be made from a variety of materials, including steel, concrete, wood, or stone, depending on the architectural design and load requirements of the building. The choice of material and design of lintels must account for factors like the span of the opening, the load being supported, and the environmental conditions.

At 4G STEEL ENTERPRISE, we specialize in providing high-quality steel lintels that are designed to meet the rigorous demands of modern construction, ensuring safety, durability, and compliance with industry standards.
